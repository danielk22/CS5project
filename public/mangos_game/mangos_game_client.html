Mangos Game Client

Get ready to play!


1. Players rotate between being the judge (who receives red cards) and normal players (who choose red cards). There is one judge per round who is given a random green card, as is displayed on the host's screen.

2. Players (except the judge) quickly click the red apple card from their hand that is best described by the word on the green apple card played by the judge.

Quick Pick Option: With five or more players, you might want to try the Quick Pick Option. The last red apple card that is selected will not be judged and is returned to that player’s hand. If you snooze, you lose!

3. After selection is finished, selected red cards will appear on the screen along with the green card. The judge selects the red card they think is best described by the word on the green apple card.

4. The winning player receives a point and play continues with another green card. Each player receives enough red cards to have seven total after each round.

5. Play continues following steps 1–4 until someone has earned enough green apple cards to win the game!
